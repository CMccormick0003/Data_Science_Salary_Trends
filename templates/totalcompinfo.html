Base Salary is just the beginning...

Consider the value of the items below to calculate your total desired compensation for a job.

What's in Total Compensation?

  • Salary or hourly rate
  • Medical benefits (eg, medical, dental)
  • Education assistance program (for employee)
  • Tuition assistance (for employee's children)
  • Flexible spending account (eg, healthcare, dependent care)
  • Retirement benefits (eg, pension plan, 401(k), 403(b))
  • Paid leave (eg, holidays, vacation, sick days,
       personal time, jury duty, bereavement, military pay)
  • Career advancement opportunities
  • Employee assistance programs
  • Life insurance
  • Disability insurance
  • Relocation assistance
  • Not on the list but important to you, ask for it
